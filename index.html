Population of countries

The first map of this assignment is the population of the countries in the world. The darkness of the color decided the population of countries in the world. As you can see from the graph, China, India, the United States have the highest population, and countries in the Middle East and North Africa have fewer people.

Population VS Land Area

The bubble plot is displaying the population versus the land area of different countries. As you can see most of the countries with a high population also have a larger land area. Besides that, most of the countries with a population between one million to 100 million. Some countries and places like Hong Kong, Macau, and Singapore have a higher population but a smaller land area. Besides that, countries in Africa and Asia usually have more people compared to other countries. Most of the European countries have less fertility rates and African countries usually have a high fertility rate.

Net Population&&Fertility Rate

In the bar chart above, the population of China is higher than any other countries but China is not the country with the most number of population increase. India has less population but a higher population increase in the year 2020. Looking at the bottom chart, China and India have a higher population increase in 2020 but the fertility rate is lower than Nigeria and Pakistan. By comparing two chats, countries with a high net population change are because of a higher population but not fertility rate.

Urban Population

From the map, I could see that there are most of the European countries, North American, South American, and some other countries generally have a higher percentage of Urban Population. These countries most likely are developed countries. For example, the US's GDP is 21427.70 billion dollars in 2019 and Australia's GDP is 1392.70 billion dollars in 2019. These show that a higher urban population may be related to better economic performance.
